How to start

How GeniusJob.AI Works Please read all before using our software

Step 1

Initial Setup:

Spend approximately one minute filling out a comprehensive form about yourself

Spend approximately one minute filling out a comprehensive form about yourself, similar to the process on Simplify Jobs. This form collects essential information including your education, work experience, skills, and any other relevant details. Resume and Preferences: Upload your most recent resume and fill out your job preferences. This includes the type of job you're looking for, desired location, any specific company preferences, and other criteria that matter to you.

Step 2

Cover Letters Attached:

For each application submitted through our platform

For each application submitted through our platform, our AI will craft a tailored and unique cover letter. This letter will be personalized based on the information provided in your resume and the specific background requirements of the role.

Step 3

Auto-Apply

Based on your profile and preferences

Based on your profile and preferences, our system will automatically identify roles and companies that match your requirements and apply on your behalf. Processing Time: Due to the high demand for our services, please allow up to 2 days for your applications to be fully processed. We appreciate your patience as we work to ensure the quality and customization of your applications.

Step 4

Application Overview:

After all applications have been submitted

After all applications have been processed, you will receive a detailed report. This report includes the roles and companies applied for, along with links to each application. Access to Report: Please note that this report will only be available after the processing of your applications has been completed. Limitations and Important Notes Application Cap: You can choose to apply to up to 200 job applications. This is the maximum limit to ensure the quality and effectiveness of each application. Ensure that the information you provide is accurate and up-to-date to maximize the effectiveness of your applications. The tailored cover letter and the strategic selection of job applications are designed to significantly increase your chances of securing interviews.
